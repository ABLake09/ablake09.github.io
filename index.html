Burt

Although Burt is in his early 40’s he looks much older from working so much as a scientist in his lab. He is short with almost grey hair and brown eyes. His hair is always sticking out for unknown reasons. Burt is very hardworking but also loves telling jokes sometimes outside of work.

Alexandra

Alexa is still quite young at 10 years old but acts mature for her age. Due to her dad Burt working most of the time she has learned to be independent but also inpatient. Alexa enjoys drawing different characters, her favorite being Pencil Dude. She has short brown hair and brown eyes. Besides drawing she also really enjoys fashion and always looks put together.

Pencil Dude

Pencil Dude looks like he came straight out of a cartoon! He stands point up on his paper thin legs. Despite his shocking appearance, Pencil Dude is very nice and is always helping others.
